About Us

Dry Oar started as two guys in the office with a dream: a dream to get away from all the hustle and bustle of the city and really live life. "One with nature and food for the worms," as granddad used to say. So, one day in 2010, we got off our rolly chairs and did something about it. We founded Dry oar Whitewater Rafting Co., and we've been loving life ever since.

We offer tour guides, gear for rafting trips, and complimentary lunches to our guests. Along our routes you'll see Colorado's amazing sights; red rock, beautiful wildlife, and so much more!
